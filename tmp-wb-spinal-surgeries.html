Spine Surgery hospitals in chennai

The Spine Institute seeks to provide advanced medical and surgical treatment for patients suffering from diseases of the spine and chronic pain. Treatment is provided via quality health services intended to restore, maintain, and improve patient wellness. The Institute employs state-of-the-art technology, modern techniques, and advanced therapies, to help patients to a pain-free lifestyle.

The department offers advanced medical and surgical treatment for patients suffering from diseases of the spine and chronic pain. It employs state-of-the-art technology, modern techniques, and advanced therapies, to help patients to a pain-free lifestyle. The institute is a comprehensive spine surgery unit and works closely with physical and occupational therapists to develop an individualized treatment plans for each patient, taking into account the diagnosis, lifestyle, and professional requirements.

The following are some of the spine surgical procedures provided at MAPIMS:

  • Minimally invasive spinal surgery
  • Endoscopic Spine Surgery
  • Cervical spine procedure
  • Anterior lumbar fusion surgery
  • Lateral lumbar fusion surgery
  • Deep Brain Stimulation
  • Diskectomy
  • Gamma Knife Radiosurgery
  • Laminectomy
  • Lumbar Puncture ( Spinal tap)
  • Spinal Fusion
  • Vagus Nerve Stimulation
  • Scoliosis
  • Kyphoplasty
